National Agency for the Development of Investment

Details of my experience and the different tasks achieved during my internship as a financial researcher.

Suspendisse quis massa vel justo

In order to fulfil my program requirement and get professional experience, I performed a thirteen-week internship in finance. My objective was to discover the financial world. I applied to an entity combining the Ministry of Industry and Finance: the National Agency for the Development of Invetsment. This agency (ANDI) has many different missions such as : Registration of investments, Promotion of investments in Algeria and abroad, Promoting territorial opportunities and potential, Facilitation of business practice, and so forth.

I wanted to work for this organisation because their trainees have to perform high quality and technical tasks. This was an opportunity to me to get involved in important national decisions and develop my technical skills.

During this internship, I have been assigned to a very specific mission, the establishment of a report concerning the distribution of investments in Algeria. The concerns that give shape to the report are: “What is the breakdown of investments in Algeria? Is there a geographical disparity in these investments? If so, what are the impacts on the Algerian economy?”. The report consists of four main parts: The socio-economic context, investment over the country, the spatial representation of investments in Algeria and the analysis of investment disparities and territorial zoning.

To achieve it, I separated my work in four different parts. Firstly, I searched for information, both qualitative and quantitative data. Secondly, I collected data, sorted them out and classified then. Furthermore, I cross-referenced investment data, using Qgis software I put them on a map of Algeria. Finally, I could draw results and write recommendations.
